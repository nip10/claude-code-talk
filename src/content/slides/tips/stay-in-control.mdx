<TipSlide
  title={"Stay in Control of the AI"}
  description={"Avoid fully autonomous loops, stay accountable for code quality, and don't fear interrupting or course correcting."}
  tags={["Workflow", "Gotcha"]}
>

- **Avoid autonomous loops:** Do not run Claude Code in fully autonomous loops without oversight. Staying hands-on and reviewing each step produces better outcomes.
- **Stay accountable:** Keep humans in the loop — do trivial edits directly, review AI output critically, and preserve deep codebase understanding. Use Claude for leverage, not abdication.
- **Interrupt freely:** It's safe and recommended to interrupt Claude, change direction, or send multiple prompts while it's executing. Press Escape, adjust, continue.

</TipSlide>
