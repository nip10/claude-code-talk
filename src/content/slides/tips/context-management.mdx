<TipSlide
  title={"Context Management: Keep It Healthy"}
  description={"Monitor context load, use a 'second brain' for lazy-loading, and bring work to the context rather than spreading it."}
  tags={["Workflow", "Performance"]}
>

- **Monitor and compact:** Use `/context` to spot heavy contributors, run `/compact` when sessions grow, and `/clear` when switching tasks. Configure the status bar to display context usage.
- **Second brain:** Maintain a local directory of project-specific context files. End of session: "Save our work to my local CLAUDE.md projects directory." Start: "Load my context from local projects."
- **Bring work to the context:** Structure workflows so relevant work accumulates in one session rather than distributing across many agents. Use lazy loading to selectively bring in only needed context.

</TipSlide>
