_Source section: Workflow & Productivity_
- **What:** Stay hands-on — avoid autonomous loops, review AI output critically, and interrupt or course-correct freely.
- **Why it matters:** The "RALPH loop" and similar patterns can produce unconvincing results. Quality failures usually come from unreviewed output and reduced ownership. Claude handles prompt queuing and interruptions gracefully.
- **How:** Do trivial edits directly, read generated diffs, reject weak code, and keep hands-on familiarity with core areas. Press Escape to interrupt, adjust your prompt, and continue — it's a normal part of the workflow.
