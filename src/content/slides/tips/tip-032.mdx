_Source section: Hooks_
- **What:** Use post-tool-use hooks to auto-format files and stop hooks to run tests/linters whenever Claude finishes.
- **Why it matters:** Ensures consistent formatting without relying on Claude, and guarantees verification never gets skipped — even when you're not watching.
- **How:** Configure a post-tool-use hook that runs your formatter (e.g., `prettier --write [file]`) on `write` and `edit` tools. Add a stop hook that executes your test or lint command at every stopping point.
