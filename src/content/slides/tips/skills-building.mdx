<TipSlide
  title={"Building and Iterating on Skills"}
  description={"Build skills from real interactions, refine through feedback cycles, and follow the six-step framework."}
  tags={["Workflow", "Prompt Technique"]}
>

- **Start from a real interaction:** Do the task manually with Claude first, then say: "This is something I do regularly. Let's turn this into a skill."
- **Feedback cycle:** Invoke the skill, observe, give targeted feedback, let Claude update `skill.md`, repeat. After 10-30 runs, output becomes highly reliable.
- **Six-step framework:** (1) Name and trigger, (2) Goal — one-sentence output, (3) Step-by-step process, (4) Reference files, (5) Rules / guardrails, (6) Self-improvement loop — plan for testing and iterating.

</TipSlide>
